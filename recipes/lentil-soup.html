Lentil Soup

Description

This is a hearty lentil soup recipe that will warm you up on a cold day or you can prepare 5 meals of this throughout the week to reheat in the microwave. I personally prepare this for my weekday dinners every day and sometimes accompany the meal with some chicken, but you should know that this recipe on its own is vegetarian.

Ingredients

Part 1 Ingredients

  • 2 Large Carrots (1.5-2 cups approx)
  • 1 Large Onion
  • 1/4 Cup of Olive Oil

Part 2 Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp Curry Powder
  • 2 Tbsp Ground Cumin
  • 1/2 tsp of Thyme
  • 4 Garlic Cloves, Minced
  • 1 28 ounce can (850 mL) of Diced Tomatoes
  • Dash of Cayenne Pepper
  • Dash of Crushed Chili Flakes

Part 3 Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Brown/Red/Green Lentils
  • 4 Cups of Vegetable Broth
  • 2 Cups of Water
  • Another Dash of Cayenne Pepper and crushed chili flakes

Part 4 Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Spinach (1 Bunch from the grocery store is fine if you are unsure)
  • 1/4 Cup of Lemon Juice

Instructions

Part 1

  1. Pour 1/4 cup of olive oil into bottom of pot. Note that pot should be enough to hold approximately 8L of liquids in this recipe. Smaller can be used.
  2. Turn on oven at medium heat.
  3. Dice up carrots into bite sized pieces.
  4. Dice up onion into 3/4 inch diced blocks.
  5. Once oil is shimmering, put carrots and onions into the pot of shimmering oil.
  6. Turn over onions and carrots with a utensil periodically.
  7. Let cook until onions begin to become translucent.

Part 2

  1. Pour Part 2 spices into pot with the cooking carrots and onions once onions begin to become translucent:
    • 1 Tbsp Curry Powder
    • 2 Tbsp Ground Cumin
    • 1/2 tsp of Thyme
    • 4 Garlic Cloves, Minced
  2. Mix spices into onions and carrots in the pot. Let sit for 2 minutes.
  3. Once you have let sit for 2 minutes, lightly drain diced tomatoes and add to pot. Stir tomatoes in briefly so onions and carrots are mixed in.
  4. Add dash of cayenne pepper and crushed chili flakes to pot and mix in.

Part 3

  1. After mixing in diced tomatoes and cayenne/crushed chili flakes, add 1 cup of washed lentils.
  2. Add in 4 cups of vegetable broth.
  3. Add in 2 cups of water.
  4. Mix soup mixture and turn up heat so that soup comes to a boil.
  5. Add second dash of cayenne pepper and chili flakes and mix in.
  6. After soup comes to a boil, let the soup simmer with the lid uncovered for 25 minutes.

Part 4

  1. Chop off the spinach stems and add the 1 cup of spinach to soup.
  2. Add 1/4 cup of lemon juice to soup.
  3. Let cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Soup is ready to serve or store for 5 days.
